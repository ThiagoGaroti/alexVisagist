VISAGISM

Visagism is an innovative concept in the world of beauty and aesthetics, focusing on the harmonious integration of one’s facial features, hairstyle, and makeup to create a balanced and personalized look.

category

Persona Reading

First, an analysis of the individual's persona is carried out, understanding their characteristics and personal goals.

Face Reading

An analysis of the individual's face is carried out, evaluating its features and shapes.

Creating A Style

An optimized and individual-oriented style will be created, something very particular and objective.

Derived from the French word “visage,” meaning face, Visagism is more than just a makeup technique; it’s an artful approach to enhancing one’s natural beauty.

At its core, Visagism takes into account an individual’s unique facial structure, bone structure, skin tone, and personal style preferences. By carefully analyzing these elements, a skilled Visagist can design a makeup and hairstyle that complements the person’s features, enhances their best qualities, and downplays any imperfections.

Visagism isn’t a one-size-fits-all approach. Instead, it’s a highly personalized process, ensuring that the chosen makeup and hairstyle align with the client’s personality, lifestyle, and overall image goals. This technique is often used in the beauty and fashion industries, helping models, actors, and everyday individuals look their best for various occasions.

In summary, Visagism is a holistic approach to beauty that empowers individuals to celebrate their uniqueness. It emphasizes the art of enhancing facial features through makeup and hairstyle, resulting in a more harmonious and self-confident appearance.

Capillary prosthesis

Capillary prosthesis, also known as a hair prosthesis or hair replacement system, is a remarkable solution for individuals dealing with hair loss or thinning hair.

Analysis

We perform an analysis of the individual and determine how the capillary prosthesis will be developed.

Implantation

The capillary prosthesis will be implanted in the necessary molds.

Maintenance

Maintenance of the hair prosthesis will be carried out to ensure that everything is perfect.

This innovative technique involves the application of a custom-made hairpiece or wig that is meticulously crafted to mimic the recipient’s natural hair in terms of color, texture, and style.

What sets capillary prosthesis apart is its ability to provide a natural and seamless appearance. These prostheses are designed to be indistinguishable from the wearer’s own hair, with the option to choose from different materials, including human hair or synthetic fibers, depending on one’s preferences and budget.

Capillary prostheses offer a range of benefits beyond their aesthetic appeal. They can boost self-confidence, restore a sense of normalcy, and allow individuals to enjoy a full head of hair without resorting to invasive surgical procedures. Maintenance and care are relatively simple, making them a practical choice for those looking for a non-permanent hair restoration solution.

Whether it’s for medical reasons, cosmetic enhancement, or personal comfort, capillary prostheses have become an invaluable resource for people looking to regain a full head of hair and enhance their overall well-being.
